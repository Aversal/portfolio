Work history

I've been a student for the majority of my life, and I haven't had a single full-time job. But that doesn't mean that I'm not a hard working person, or that I haven't ever worked on anything.
Most of my work history consists of either short summer job ventures or voluntary work. The paid gigs happened a long time ago, and they include mostly working on youth camps and painting jobs, so that's not so important. So instead, I'd like to tell a bit more about the game industry related voluntary work I've done.




Kajak Games



From May 2019 to May 2020, I worked as the Event Manager of Kajak Games.

Jack of Some Trades

I'm a game development student with a passion for game design, level design, animation and sound design. I also have some proficiency in graphical arts, composing for games and working as a team lead.

Currently I am enrolled in the Kajaani University of Applied Sciences in Finland, and I'm looking for internship opportunities. I can work in any of the roles mentioned above, or even take on new ones if need be. I'm willing to work more or less anywhere in Europe, so don't hesistate to contact me if you'd like to have an intern who won't disappoint you!
